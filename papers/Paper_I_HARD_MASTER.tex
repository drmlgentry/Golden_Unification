\title{Discrete Logarithmic Structure in Standard Model Flavor Data}
\author{Marvin Gentry}
\date{\today}

\begin{document}

\begin{abstract}
We examine Standard Model flavor observables in logarithmic coordinates under a fixed anchoring
convention and report a reproducible discrete organization: fermion masses and mixing parameters
cluster near integer-lattice locations within bounded residuals. The emphasis is empirical and
auditable: all numerical outputs are generated deterministically and validated against explicit
null hypotheses. Interpretation is sharply delimited; methodological implementation details are
externalized to a companion methods paper.
\end{abstract}
